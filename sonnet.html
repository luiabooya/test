An Ode to David



This sonnet is for, David, my sleepy bear lobster poop bunny. He is the sexiest man of all time, and he is a brilliant, wise, strong, kind, generous, loving, and righteous man!

I am the luckiest squeaky bunny.



It is time to write

Haikus that are full of love

For my sweet David



How handsome is the face that is my love's--

His boyish smile just sets my heart abuzz!

How happy can this lucky lady be?

I'll celebrate for all eternity!

So great a man that it seems hardly sane;

He's Superman and I'm his Lois Lane.
